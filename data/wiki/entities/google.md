# Google

A global technology company and leader in AI research, known for developing the Transformer architecture and the BERT model.

## Overview

Google's AI research, primarily through Google Brain and DeepMind, has produced some of the most influential work in modern machine learning. Their focus spans from fundamental architecture (Transformers) to large-scale pre-trained models (BERT, PaLM, Gemini).

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Transformer Architecture:** Introduced the self-attention mechanism, replacing RNNs/CNNs for sequence modeling.
- **BERT:** Pioneered bidirectional pre-training for language understanding.
- **TensorFlow:** Developed one of the most widely used ML frameworks.

## Connections

Google is the developer of [[bert]] and the authors of the seminal paper [[vaswani-2017-attention]].
