# RACE

Reading Comprehension Dataset from Examinations. A benchmark consisting of English exams from middle and high schools in China.

## Overview

RACE is designed to be more challenging than SQuAD because it requires deeper reasoning and a more holistic understanding of the text, as the answers are multiple-choice rather than spans.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Complex Reasoning:** Requires the model to synthesize information across multiple sentences.
