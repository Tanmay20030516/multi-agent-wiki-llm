# OpenAI

An AI research and deployment company focused on ensuring that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity.

## Overview

OpenAI is best known for the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) series of models, which shifted the paradigm of NLP toward large-scale generative pre-training.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **GPT Series:** Developed the first widely successful generative pre-trained models.
- **RLHF:** Advanced the use of reinforcement learning from human feedback for alignment.

## Connections
