# GPT

Generative Pre-trained Transformer. A family of models that use a decoder-only Transformer architecture for generative language modeling.

## Overview

Introduced by OpenAI, GPT demonstrated that pre-training a model on a massive corpus of unlabeled text using a next-token prediction objective allows the model to perform well on diverse downstream tasks.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Generative Pre-training:** Established the effectiveness of the pre-train then fine-tune paradigm.
- **Zero-shot potential:** Showed that large models can perform tasks without explicit fine-tuning.
