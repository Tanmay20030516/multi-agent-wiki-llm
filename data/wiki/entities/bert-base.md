# BERT-Base

The base variant of the BERT model.

## Overview

BERT-Base is the smaller of the two primary configurations introduced in the original BERT paper, designed for efficiency while maintaining strong performance.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Architecture:** 12 layers (L=12), 768 hidden size (H=768), 12 attention heads (A=12).

## Connections

A specific configuration of [[bert]] described in [[devlin-2019-bert]].
