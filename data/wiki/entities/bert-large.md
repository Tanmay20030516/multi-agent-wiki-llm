# BERT-Large

The large variant of the BERT model.

## Overview

BERT-Large is the high-capacity version of BERT, achieving state-of-the-art results on most benchmarks.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Architecture:** 24 layers (L=24), 1024 hidden size (H=1024), 16 attention heads (A=16).
