# GLUE

General Language Understanding Evaluation. A collection of diverse natural language understanding tasks used to evaluate the generalizability of language models.

## Overview

GLUE is a benchmark designed to provide a more comprehensive evaluation of NLU models than single-task benchmarks. It consists of several tasks, including sentiment analysis, textual entailment, and similarity.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Standardized Evaluation:** Provides a single score (average accuracy) across multiple tasks to measure general linguistic competence.

## Connections

Used as a primary evaluation benchmark for [[bert]] in [[devlin-2019-bert]].
