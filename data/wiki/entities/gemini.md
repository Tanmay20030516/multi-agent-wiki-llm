# Gemini

A family of multimodal AI models developed by Google.

## Overview

Gemini is designed to be natively multimodal, meaning it was trained on text, images, audio, and video from the start, rather than having separate modalities bolted on.

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Native Multimodality:** Seamlessly handles multiple input and output types.
- **Performance:** Competes with other flagship models in reasoning and coding.
