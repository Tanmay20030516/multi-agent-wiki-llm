# SQuAD

Stanford Question Answering Dataset. A benchmark for reading comprehension where models must answer questions based on a given passage of text.

## Overview

SQuAD consists of question-answer pairs where the answer to every question is a segment of text from the corresponding reading passage. It has two main versions: v1.1 (all questions are answerable) and v2.0 (some questions are unanswerable).

## Key Contributions / Capabilities

- **Reading Comprehension:** Challenges models to locate precise answer spans within a context.

## Connections

BERT achieved state-of-the-art results on both SQuAD v1.1 and v2.0, as described in [[devlin-2019-bert]].
